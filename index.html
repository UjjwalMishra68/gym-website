Frequently asked questions
Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we provide personalized training sessions designed to meet your fitness goals and schedule. Our experienced trainers will work closely with you to create a customized workout plan tailored to your needs.

What are your office hours?

Our Gym is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Are group classes included in the membership?

Yes, our membership includes access to a variety of group fitness classes. Join us for dynamic sessions led by experienced instructors that cater to different fitness levels and interests. Enjoy the camaraderie and motivation of group workouts as part of your membership benefits.

Do you have facilities for beginners?

Absolutely! We offer facilities and programs specifically designed to accommodate beginners. Our supportive environment ensures that everyone, regardless of experience level, can comfortably begin their fitness journey with us.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial period for you to experience our facilities and classes firsthand. It's an opportunity to explore our services, meet our instructors, and get a sense of our community atmosphere before making a commitment. We encourage you to take advantage of this trial to see how our offerings can support your fitness goals.

What should I bring on my first visit?

On your first visit, please bring comfortable workout attire, athletic shoes, a water bottle to stay hydrated, and any necessary personal items such as a towel or yoga mat. If you haven't completed our registration forms online, please arrive a few minutes early to fill them out.

How do I cancel my membership?

To cancel your membership, please visit our front desk or contact our customer service department during business hours. We'll assist you through the cancellation process and answer any questions you may have. Depending on our cancellation policy, there may be specific requirements or a notice period to fulfill.
